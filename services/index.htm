New Construction & Remodels

New construction and remodels are our jam! Fine Art Interiors excels at overcoming the unique and complex challenges of these types of projects. We come up with great design solutions to add to the functionality of your home and make sense for your overall investment and timeline.

We understand that the new build and remodel process can be overwhelming and daunting. That’s why we use project management software as well as construction site apps to help organize the tedious details and manage timelines so you can relax and enjoy the process knowing we’ve got you covered!

We provide guided checklists and client portals you can log into and view the real-time progress of your project so you never feel in the dark. And, of course, you’ll hear from us personally each week with a project update.

Let us handle all the details so you can actually enjoy the process.

Designer for a Day

(Gourmet lunch included)

Enjoy your VIP day with this exclusive Interior Designer.  We invest a day with you to help tackle some of your most pressing design challenges.  We will spend a design-filled morning and afternoon with you giving you fresh ideas for color, style, and organization.  Our goal is to ensure your selected room meets its full potential.  We include a delicious gourmet boxed lunches from a local caterer so you can enjoy the 6 hour session fully without interruption.

What to expect during the 6hr in home session:

  • First we take a tour of your home to identify the design challenges in the selected areas and your goals for the space
  • Decide on design concept and approach
  • Establish color scheme and paint colors
  • Provide quick floor plan sketches, move existing pieces to achieve the desired look and feel
  • Help select furniture and or materials
  • Styling with existing accessories
  • Specify new pieces if needed

Let’s schedule a 30 minute discovery call to tell me a bit about your space and your goals for your space. We’ll email you an in-depth design questionnaire to give us an inside look at your décor aesthetic, your style, pattern, and your all-important color preferences.

This service is a perfect fit for you if:

  • You would like Kimberly’s expertise and design talent to devise the best plan for your space that you can execute on your own
  • You want expert design assistance in pulling together a room
  • You want an experienced design expert to vet all your design decisions

Investment:

Our Designer & Lunch for a Day package is $1550 for a 6-hour session.

Our Process

(For Full Service Design & New Construction & Remodel Projects)

Inquiry + Discovery

  • Engage you in the design process by asking a series of questions to help define goals and objectives for your project
  • Explore your overall style and aesthetic
  • Create efficiencies for you in your newly designed home
  • Establish the project timeline and overall investment

Concept + Planning

  • Take site measurements
  • Develop design concepts, create floorplans and layouts
  • Develop 3 Dimensional drawings
  • Select materials for hard surfaces, cabinetry, flooring, furnishings, fabrics and fixtures
  • Create design boards with product images and inspiration
  • Presentation of design approach for client approval

Proposals

  • Build your project team
  • Meet with trades to request estimates for work required
  • Review the bids with you for final selection of contractors
  • Price material finishes, furnishings and fixtures

Finalize Design + Planning

  • Confirm contractor selections
  • Verify necessary design revisions
  • Secure material selections and furnishings for client approval

Implementation + Execution

  • Process orders for materials, furnishings and fixtures. 
  • Online client portal that allows you to login and access furniture specs, pricing and check the status of orders throughout the course of your project
  • We make scheduled site visits to check for progress and accuracy of designs during the remodel or new build process
  • Organize deliveries and product inspections for construction related orders

Installation + Big Reveal

  • Organize delivery of furnishings
  • Execute installation of all furnishings and styling for each space.
  • THE BIG REVEAL!!
  • Celebrate and enjoy your newly designed space!
