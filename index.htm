Our Philosophy

At Fine Art Interiors, we’re driven to create environments our clients love coming home to. That’s why the homes we design are one-of-a-kind havens of beauty, comfort, and serenity - dream spaces created just for you, and crafted to enhance your lifestyle at home.

You’re in Good Hands!

Trust is a vital part of any designer-client relationship. After all, it’s your home you’re placing in our care! That responsibility is something we don’t take lightly. We’re committed to honoring your style and providing you with spaces that look and feel personal, unique, and original - just like you!

Hi, I'm Kimberly!

As an expert in interior design and owner of Fine Art Interiors, my aim is to provide you with a look you’ll not only love, but that’s also truly and uniquely yours. Solving your design challenges, maximizing your home’s space, and providing custom storage and organizational solutions are the cherry on top! My team and I offer experience and expertise to handle large-scale remodels and new construction, as well as refurnishing projects in the Tucson area and beyond.

Let’s talk about how we can make your home the sanctuary you’ve always wanted!
